AyahBlocks 📖
AyahBlocks is a modern, web-based Hifz (memorization) assistant designed to help users master the Holy Quran through a scientifically-backed, multi-sensory approach. By combining visual breakdown and auditory repetition, it caters to both visual and auditory learners for more effective retention.

✨ Features
9-Tile Visual Breakdown: Verses are intelligently divided into 9 interactive "blocks." This method helps visual learners memorize small chunks at a time by clicking to hide/reveal text.

1x–100x Repetition Slider: An integrated auditory loop system allows for up to 100 repetitions per ayah or range—a perfect tool for those who learn faster through hearing.

Integrated Info Bar: Stay oriented with real-time data for every verse, including:

Juz number

Hizb designation

Page (with Left/Right indicator)

Line number

Multi-Reciter Support: Choose from world-renowned reciters to perfect your Tajweed and pronunciation.

Custom Range Playback: Select a single Ayah or a specific range of verses to play and repeat in sequence.

Multilingual Support: Includes translations in English, Indonesian, Bengali, Urdu, Tamil, and Turkish for better understanding in your native language.

Dynamic Playback Speed: Adjustable audio speed from 0.5x to 3.0x.

Modern Interface: Responsive design with Right-to-Left (RTL) support, Dark Mode, and quick-share social buttons.

🚀 How It Works
Select Your Reciter & Language: Choose your preferred voice and translation for better comprehension.

Pick a Surah & Ayah: Navigate to the specific portion you wish to memorize.

Use the Blocks: Click on the 9 tiles to blur/unblur text, testing your visual memory.

Loop for Mastery: Set the repetition slider (up to 100x) and playback range to solidify your memorization through hearing.

🛠️ Technology Stack
HTML5 & CSS3: For a clean, responsive, and themed UI.

JavaScript (Vanilla): For real-time API integration and playback logic.

Quran.com API: Powers the verse data, translations, and word-by-word breakdowns.

Al Quran Cloud API: Provides high-quality audio streaming from multiple reciters.
